Ermine Cooperative

How Ermine Cooperative

Ermine Cooperative is a concept of Ermine Nig. Ltd geared towards empwering business owners. Amongst the many benefits of the program, Ermine Nig Ltd is committed to encouraging small to medium scale business who signed up on the ErmineApp to enjoy the following benefits;

Benefits:
  • Access to Financial Services:
    Ermine provides access to loans ans savings facilities with a more favorable terms than the traditional cooperative society or banks.
  • Economic Empowerment:
    It is an avenue for participating merchant to pool resources in collective economic activities, and improve livelihoods
  • Education & Training:
    Ermine offers education and training programs to its members, enhancing their skills and knowledge in entrepreneurship
  • Access To Markets:
    Ermine Cooperative facilitates access to markets for its members' products and Services enabling them to obtain better prices and increase their income.
