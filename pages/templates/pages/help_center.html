Merchant: FAQs

What you need to know about Ermine Merchant access

Who is a merchant?
A merchant is a business owner or service provider e.g trader, shop owners, welder. painter or anyone with daily cash transaction.
What Does Ermine Offers
Ermine application connects the merchants with people in need of CASH, this helps the merchant to dispense his/her available cash and get a transfer from the ermine subscriber in turn. The benefit of this is that the stress of going on a long queue in the bank for deposit is removed.
What Other Benefits?
Instead of the stress and transport expenses to get to the bank, Ermine merchants earn extra Income as commission/charges paid on every transaction.
How To Initiate Transaction
Go to the MENU area and Click Merchant Transact, -> select "Set Transaction", follow the procedure to complete the form.
How To Pay Company Charges
Ermine Merchant pays the company charges before the transaction is made ACTIVE to Subscribers. Once the payment is confirmed by the company the merchant becomes visible to prospective subscribers for patronage.
